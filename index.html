My Latest Adventure

Published on June 6, 2023 by Eric Truong

My girlfriend and I recently took a vacation to Los Angles, California to visit family. I have already been to California many times when I was younger. Now that I am all grown up, I get to go where I want to go. We had an extra day, so we decided to go visit a national park in the heart of California. Sequioa National Park is where nature's ancient wonders stood tall and proud. The prospect of walking amidst the colossal trees and exploring the beauty filled us with excitement. However, it was an 8-hour drive from LA to the park. We left early in the morning and once we finally got there. It was tiring because we also had to wait an hour to get in the park since everyone went too but it was worth it. Hiking along the well-marked paths, I marveled at the enormity of the trees. I reached out and touched the rough bark, feeling a connection to the past and an appreciation for nature's enduring strength. I was able to take the picture standing on a huge boulder and I am now in love with the picture for a lifetime.
